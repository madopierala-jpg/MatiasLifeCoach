About me

I'm Matias.

I know what it feels like to carry fear and self-doubt while trying to figure out who you are and where you're going. My own journey — through identity, reinvention, and learning to stand in my truth — is what brought me to coaching.

I work with people navigating transitions: coming out, changing careers, rebuilding confidence, healing from what's held them back. Life and professional growth aren't separate — when one part of you shifts, everything else moves too.

If you're queer, trans, questioning, or simply someone who wants to grow — you belong here. This is a space built on empathy, not judgment.

What I help with

Three areas. One conversation.

We start wherever you are. These are the spaces I work in most — and they overlap more than you'd think.

01

Fear & Self-Doubt

The inner critic, imposter syndrome, the voice that says "not yet" every time you try to grow. We'll gently untangle where it comes from — and build a new one.

02

Confidence & Identity

Career & Purpose

You deserve work that aligns with who you are. From career pivots to leading with authenticity — let's design a path that actually feels like yours.
